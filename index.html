Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, security, and processes.

Are the accounts truly verified and safe to use?

Yes, all our accounts are fully verified with proper identification documents and have been aged for 6-36 months. They include 2FA protection and are configured with anti-detection measures. We provide a 7-day replacement guarantee if any issues arise.

What happens if my account gets restricted or banned?

We offer a 7-day replacement guarantee for any account issues. Additionally, our Account Verification & Recovery service has an 80% success rate for recovering restricted/banned accounts. We also provide guidance on best practices to minimize restriction risks.

How quickly can I get started after ordering?

For standard services, delivery is within 2-4 hours during business hours. For premium and custom packages, we typically deliver within 24 hours. We offer expedited delivery (within 1 hour) for urgent requests at an additional 20% fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT), bank transfers, PayPal, and credit cards. For larger orders, we offer payment plans and custom billing arrangements. All payments are secured with encryption.

Do you offer custom solutions for agencies?

Yes, we work with many digital agencies and offer custom packages including white-label solutions, dedicated account managers, volume discounts, and custom billing. Contact us to discuss your specific agency needs.

Is my data and information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We have strict confidentiality policies and never share client information with third parties. We use encrypted communication channels and secure data storage. Confidentiality agreements are available upon request for enterprise clients.
